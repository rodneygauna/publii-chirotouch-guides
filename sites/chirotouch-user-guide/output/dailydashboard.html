Daily Dashboard

The Daily dashboard will be the default tab that opens when navigating to the Dashboard area. It has seven visuals that focus on the current day. The default time range is the current day (Start of Day - End of Day).  But it can be changed to other days to suit your needs.

Appointments by Provider

Shows the appointment counts grouped by Provider for active, checked in, and checked out appointments. Allows you to see the total of appointments by the provider and compare across all practice providers.

Appointments by Status

Shows appointment counts grouped by Appointment Start Time and sub-grouped by Appointment Status. Helpful to see hour-by-hour breakdowns on different days. The default is hourly. 

Charges by Billing Codes

Shows the charges and their volume grouped by Bill Type and sub-grouped by billing Code.  It gives you the ability to see what codes are used the most.

Payments by Method

Gives the total calculated payments (by method) grouped by Payment Type.

Encounters by Provider

This table groups encounters by the provider.  See what appointments the provider has if an encounter has been started and which encounters are signed.

Encounter Diagnosis

This bar graph shows the diagnosis codes used across all encounters.  It gives you an excellent comparison of codes to see which codes are used most.

Encounter Charges

This bar graph shows the total charge codes used in encounters. You can go into the details to see which encounters have been posted to the ledger and the dollar amounts.
