Patient Flow

Patient Flow allows you to easily monitor the flow of patients scheduled for the day and quickly take action for the patient appointment with the handy drag feature.  Also, from Patient Flow, you can add a Walk-in Patient, Add New Appointment and print a Daily Schedule. 

Today's Appointments

The Today's Appointment column is all the appointments for the day for the provider(s) selected. You can drag an appointment from Today's Appointment to the Patients Checked In column.

The patient Check In slideout will appear for you to verify the billing details, add a case or authorization, then Complete Check In.

Patients Checked In

From the Patients Checked In column, you can click on a patient.

The slideout will open to update the appointment or Check Out the patient.

Patients Seen Today

Once a patient is Checked Out, they will move to the Patients Seen Today column.

Providers

Choose which providers whose schedules you wish to see.

Print Daily Schedule

Print a daily schedule.

Print a daily schedule for individual or multiple providers.

Daily Patient Totals

Use the Daily Patient Totals to get a snapshot of the day.

  • Total Appointments - all appointments (for today) (does not include deleted)

  • New Patients - unique, unmatched appointments plus patients without an appointment checkout before today

  • Scheduled - total appointments  (does not include walk-ins, canceled or no show) 

  • Walk-In - appointments scheduled as a walk-in patient

  • Kept - total appointments (does not include rescheduled, no show, and canceled) 

  • Rescheduled - rescheduled appointments (for today)

  • Canceled - canceled appointments (for today)

  • No Show - appointments marked as no show (for today)

  • Checked In - all checked in patients (for today)

  • Checked Out - all checked out patients (for today)
