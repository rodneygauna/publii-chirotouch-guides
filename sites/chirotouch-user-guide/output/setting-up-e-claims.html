Setting Up Electronic Claims

The Claims Worklist is a convenient way for billers to manage all claims, whether paper or electronic submissions. The Claims Worklist allows users to view a snapshot of claims in the following categories.

Create Invoice For A Primary Electronic Claim

1. To generate an electronic claim, the selected payer must be configured to submit electronic claims. If you have not already done this:

  • Go to Payer Plan Maintenance
  • Pick the Payer you would like to update
  • From the Update Payer Plan slideout, click on the Claim Submission tab
  • Update the As Primary Payer and/or As Secondary Payer dropdowns to Electronic Claims
  • Save your update

Electronic Claim Notifications

The collapsible menu appears when Notifications, Warnings, or Errors are displayed.

1. For electronic claims, the system will display the overall status of a claim as a notification in the menu (top left of the claim form). 

2. Click the down arrow to display the most current claim status.  Service line level status codes and descriptions will display in tooltips to the left of specific charges in box 24 of the Claim form.  A single status can contain two lines: One for a Status Category Code and one for a Status Code. NOTE:  To learn more about Status Category Codes and Status Codes, visit https://x12.org/codes/.

 3. A link will display in the collapsible menu when service line level statuses are available. 

4. Clicking the link will scroll down to box 24.

5. Clicking Status Codes will display the code and description in the tooltip.

Change Claim Status

You can change the status of electronic claims.  Select the claim(s) in the Claims Worklist.  This example is from Denied & Rejected.

You can either put a checkmark for the claim(s) and then uses the ellipsis to select Change Status or select an individual claim number.

If you selected Change Status from the ellipsis, you can update the status for multiple claims.

When you select the individual claim number, it will open View Claim.  Then use the Status dropdown menu to manually change the claim's status.

Note: Claims that have a status of ERA - Received or Validation Error (from a Clearinghouse) may not be changed. 

History Log

The History Log shows all statuses for a claim throughout its lifecycle, for both paper and electronic claims. It displays the date and time the status was assigned, the status, and the description.

1. To access the History Log, click the ellipsis on the upper right of the screen and select View History Log. 

2. The most recent status is at the top.

Verifying CHC Enrollment

Your enrollment through CHC must be AUTHORIZED to submit and receive electronic claims.

To ensure your authorization is complete, go to Change Healthcare and log into your account.  Search Submitted Enrollments.

You can view the Status for each payer to make sure you are set up and ready to submit electronic claims successfully.
