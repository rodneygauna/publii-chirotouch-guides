Welcome to ChiroTouch

This is the ChiroTouch User Guide, a step-by-step manual used to deliver general information, instructions, and helpful tips related to ChiroTouch.  It is always available to you.  You can bookmark it for quick reference.  If you would like to see the latest changes and updates in the program, visit What's New.

We recommend starting first with the Getting Started section. Here, you'll learn how to log in, log out, reset passwords, and gain a general overview of ChiroTouch.

This is only one of the options available to you 24/7 to help you learn and master ChiroTouch.  You also have the Resource Center and ChiroTouch Community.

Resource Center

You'll also find throughout your ChiroTouch software that we've provided tooltips and a handy Resource Center (the question mark at the bottom of your screen) to help answer your questions and familiarize yourself with the software.

Note: To ensure that you can utilize these tooltips and resources, be sure to disable any pop-up blockers and browser extensions.

From the Resource Center, you can get to your Client ID, find out how to reach out if you need help, view walkthrough videos, product updates, videos & courses.

ChiroTouch Community

You will need to log in to become a part of the ChiroTouch Community.

Here, we have many choices to meet your need for help 24/7.  

  • Use the Search bar to query our knowledgebase for articles, videos, and courses 
  • Select from our Featured Topics to look through articles of interest
  • Ask Mabel, our chatbot, your questions. She’ll connect you with a person if she doesn’t know the answer
  • Become part of Discussions with other ChiroTouch users
  • Open a Support case within ChiroTouch Community 

 Live Support Hours are Monday through Friday, 7AM – 5PM Pacific time.
