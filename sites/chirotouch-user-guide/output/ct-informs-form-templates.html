CT InForms - Form Templates

Form Templates

CT InForms comes with a variety of pre-built Questionnaires, Consent and Other Forms.  You can duplicate the forms and personalize them to fit your practices' needs.

Note: Only the administrator of the CT Informs account may delete questionnaires and forms.

Questionnaires

CT Informs comes with a variety of standard questionnaires for your patients to fill out.  

Consent and Other Forms

Forms can contain places to sign or initial.  They also may have areas to fill in detailed information.  Multiple forms can be sent with each questionnaire.

Create Custom Forms

To make additions, remove questions, add consent forms, or make any other changes, Duplicate a questionnaire as your starting point and rename it. 

Duplicate Questions from One Questionnaire to Add to Another 

You can copy questions from one and add them to another. 

  1. Open the questionnaire that has the questions you want to copy.
  2. Select the questions you want to copy by checking the box to the left of each question. 
  3. Click Copy To and select the questionnaire you'd like to copy the questions. 

Adding Questions from Scratch 

To add a new question, click on the large green Add New Question.  

You can select from these Question Options. 

You'll find numerous choices for Question Type to add to your forms. Hover over the example link to show you a sample of what that question type can do. 

Rearrange Questions 

During the questionnaire creation or editing process, you may want to move your questions up and down. 

  • Main Questions:  Click on the three bars on the right of the question and drag it where you want it. 
  • Items Within Questions:  Click on the three bars on the right of the question and drag it where you want it. 

Add Conditional Rules

Sometimes some sections are conditional based on previous patient responses. For example, if you have a female-only section, you can use Rules to show these questions only if the patient selected female as their gender. 

  • Select Add New Rule and set the parameters of the rule 
  • Edit existing rules by clicking the blue pencil button to the right of the rule 
  • Delete a rule by clicking on the red button with an x in it 

Add a Drawing Canvas

1. Select the form that you'd like to add the canvas.

2. Click Drawing Canvas from the navigation.

3. Left-click on your mouse twice on the body image (or another spot that you'd like to be able to draw upon) to enable the drawing canvas (blue rectangle).

4. Now manipulate the drawing canvas (blue rectangle) to cover the area you want available for patients to draw.

5. Save it.  

Send More Than One Form at Once

If you want to send more than one form to a patient at once, you can do that by creating a package that includes one questionnaire with all the forms that should be sent together.  

NOTE:  Only one Questionnaire can be sent at a time.  

Create A Package

1. Navigate to My Forms and click on the desired Questionnaire.

2. Click on the Consent/Other Forms button on the toolbar.

3. Check the forms you want to attach to the Questionnaire (they will appear in the order marked).

4. Click Save.

Patient Intake with Write Back

The Patient Intake form will write back directly to the demographics and insurance of the Patient record.  Once patients send in their form, the information will go directly into ChiroTouch.  Review forms to see what information changed.

If you open the Patient Intake (v2) and select demographics or insurance questions, you can see where the questions map to by selecting the blue gear.

These are the specific fields that will write back to CT with the correct Type & Profile Mapping selected.

  • Patient Demographics
      • First Name
      • Last Name
      • Middle Name
      • DOB
      • Sex at birth
      • Marital status
      • Employment status
      • Street 1
      • Street 2
      • Zip
      • State
      • Mobile Phone
      • Home Phone
      • Work Phone
      • Email 
  • Insurance
      • Payer
      • Payer Plan
      • Policy Number
      • Group ID
      • Relationship to Patient
      • Insured First & Last Name
      • Insured DOB

Patient Intake Insurance Information

1. In order for the insurance information to write back properly, first the Payer Plan must be enabled in ChiroTouch. Only Payer Plans that have been enabled will show in the dropdown list of the intake form.

2.  Patients must select from one of the payers in the dropdown or select UNLISTED.

3.  If UNLISTED is selected, the patient can take a picture of their insurance card to send with their intake form.  The office can then manually add the payer information.

Patient Intake with Write Back (customers before April 1)

If you are a current CT InForms customer before April 1, you will need to import the new Patient Intake (v2) to get the write back for patient demographics and insurance information.

1.  Go to Standard Form.  Find Patient Intake (v2) and select Import.

2. You should then Deactivate the original Patient Intake to take advantage of the write back to patient demographics and insurance.
