Merchant Account Services 

 
In the Merchant Account Services section, you can set up merchant accounts and card readers to process debit and credit card payments. Below you'll learn how to add merchant account accounts and card readers. 
 
Note: This section requires assistance from Global Payments (OpenEdge) to help configure merchant account and card reader details for your practice account.

Add Merchant Account

1.  Select Settings.

2.  Select Billing Settings.
3.  Select Merchant Account Services.

4.  Select ADD MERCHANT ACCOUNT (in blue).

5.  From the Add Merchant Account slideout, with the assistance of Global Payments, fill out the required information below:

  • Merchant Account Name 
  • MOTO Service account - This account service type is used for Mail Order/Telephone Order payments when a card is not present. Global Payments (OpenEdge) will provide you with your account details and will help configure your merchant account for your practice. Contact Global Payments (OpenEdge) to update your merchant account details.
    • XWeb ID
    • Terminal ID
    • AuthKey
  • Retail Service Account - This account service type is used for payments with a card reader when a card is present. Global Payments (OpenEdge) will provide you with your account details and will help configure your merchant account for your practice. Contact Global Payments (OpenEdge) to update your merchant account details.
    • XWeb ID
    • Terminal ID
    • AuthKey 

6.  Select SAVE MERCHANT ACCOUNT (in blue).

Add A Card Reader

1.  Select Settings.

2.  Select Billing Settings.
3.  Select Merchant Account Services.

4.  Select Card Readers, followed by Add Card Reader (in blue).

5.  From the Add Card Reader slideout, with the assistance of Global Payments, fill out the required information below.

6.  Select SAVE CARD READER (in blue).
