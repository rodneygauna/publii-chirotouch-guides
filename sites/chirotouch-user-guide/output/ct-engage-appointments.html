CT Engage - Appointments

Appointments contain your appointment statuses, settings to manage your appointments, and a message log so you can see the messages that have been sent in one convenient place. 

Overview

The Overview tab is a quick view of the Unconfirmed, Newly Confirmed, and Appointments where a Reschedule is Needed. 

Unconfirmed

Unconfirmed contains the unconfirmed patients on the calendar in the next three days. Click on one of the dates/days to see a list of all unconfirmed patients scheduled for the day.

Newly Confirmed

Newly Confirmed lists patients that have recently confirmed their appointment. This is a list of all patients that have confirmed their appointment through text or email.

Every time a patient confirms their appointment, you'll get an email confirmation alert.

Reschedule Needed

Patients that are marked as a No Show will appear in missed appointments.  If they do not have another appointment on the schedule, that will be indicated.

Schedule Tab

Schedule shows you how many appointments you have on a given day, along with the patient's phone number and email, if available.

Opted Out Notification

If you see an appointment on the schedule with an Opted Out Status, your patient has notified the system that they no longer wish to receive texts or emails from CT Engage.  Click here to learn more about the Opt Out list.

Missed Appointments

The Missed Appointments feature shows the patient information when they missed an appointment on the day after their scheduled appointment.  It prompts them to call or text your office to reschedule.

Patients will be prompted to call or text your office to reschedule.

All messages between your office and your patients will show in the Patient Communicator.

A notification will also show in the Appointments Schedule to show that a message was sent.

Settings

In Settings, you'll find the ability to change the messages you send to your patient under two tabs: Appointment Reminders and Appointment Confirmations.

Appointment Reminders

Add variables to your text and email appointment reminders.

Appointment Confirmations

Add variables to your text and email appointment confirmations. Choose what you'd like to send when an appointment has been scheduled more than seven days before the appointment.  You can also change the confirmation request reminders sent when the patient appointment falls in the 3-7 day window and has not yet been confirmed.

Message Log

The Message Log displays the last 30 days of automated reminder messages.
