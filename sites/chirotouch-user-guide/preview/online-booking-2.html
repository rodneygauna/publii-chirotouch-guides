Online Booking 

In Online Booking under Practice Settings, you can set up policies to control how and when patients will be able to book, reschedule and cancel online. These policies will apply to online booking for all appointment types and providers. 

Note: Your site works across all phones, tablets, and computers.

Set Up Your Online Booking Site

1.  Select Settings.

2.  Select Practice Settings.
3.  Select Online Booking.

4.  Set up Booking Details highlighted below: 

Use the Direct Link to your Online Booking

Direct Links can be pasted in email signatures, and/or displayed on your practice website.

  • Direct Link - Link used to route your patients to your online booking site, which can be copied and pasted
  • COPY BOOKING SITE LINK - Copies your online booking link allowing you to paste

Add a Schedule Appointment Button to your Website

Use the COPY HTML button if you'd like to add a "Schedule Appointment Button" to your website, which directs your patients to an online booking site. 

New Appointment Policies 

  • How far in advance can a patient book - allows you to choose how far in advance patients can schedule appointments
  • How much advance notice time do you require for new appointments? - allows you to determine how soon your patients can re-book new appointments.  

Rescheduling and Cancellation Policies 

  • Cut off time for allowing patients to cancel or reschedule online - allows you to choose how far in advance patients can cancel or reschedule appointments online
  • Client policy notice to appear on booking site and email notifications - displays a message on your online booking site and email notifications.

Note: The below information should be shared with your IT department or individuals who help manage your practice website.

5.  Select SAVE (in blue). 

What Invite Looks Like to Your Customer

1.  The customer will get an invite in their email.

2.  When the customer clicks on Create Account, they will be directed to your online booking portal.

Note: Only appointments that Allow Patients to Book Online will be shown to patients.

3.  After a patient schedules an appointment online, it will show up in scheduling. Learn more about how to send an email to a patient from their profile.

Patient Experience Online Booking Site

Here is what your patients will see and be able to do from the Online Booking Site.

Patient Not Logged In

If a patient tries to check in for an appointment but is not logged in, the patient will see Appointment Check In, Walk-In Appointment, and Book an Appointment.

1. Appointment Check In - patients can check in up to 1 hour before their appointment. Patients not logged in must provide their Name and Date of Birth to look up their appointment.

Upcoming appointments that fall within the one-hour time may be checked in.

2. Walk-In Appointment - patients can schedule themselves for an appointment immediately. 

NOTE: This feature must be enabled in Self Check In.

3. Book An Appointment - patients can book an appointment for a future date.

Patient Logged In

When the patient is logged in, they will see Appointment Check In, Walk-In Appointment, Book an Appointment, and Manage My Appointments.

1. Appointment Check In - patients can check in up to 1 hour before their appointment.

2. Walk-In Appointment - patients can schedule themselves for an appointment immediately.  This feature must be enabled in Self Check In.

3. Book An Appointment - patients can book an appointment for a future date.

4.  Manage My Appointments - patients can see their past and upcoming appointments and book new appointments.
