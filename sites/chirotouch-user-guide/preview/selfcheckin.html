Self Check In

The Self Check In feature gives your practice added flexibility.  You can use the tools to set up your patients for online check in or to print a QR Code to use when they walk into your office.

Appointment Check In

Patients do not need to log in to use Appointment Check In, but they must fill out their name and date of birth to match their upcoming appointment in the patient roster.  Patients may only use Self Check In one hour in advance of their scheduled appointment.

1.  Patients will need to fill in their name and date of birth to LOOK UP their appointment.

2.  Once a match is found, appointments available for Self CHECK IN are shown.

3. The checked in button will dim when the process is successful.

No Matching Appointment Found

If no matching appointment is found for the information the patient entered, patients will receive the following message and options.

QR Code

A printable QR Code will open in a new window to allow easy printing of QR code.  Hang in your office for a contactless Check In.

Enable (unchecked)

By default, Enable patient check in is unchecked.

When Enable is unchecked, patients move to the Arrived status. 

Note: The text for these appointments is italicized to draw your attention to them.

On the Update Appointment card, you'll see Appointment Status is now Arrived if they used the Self Check In feature.

Enable Check In Online

To enable this feature, check the box.

When a patient uses the Self Check In, you'll see the patient moved to Patients Checked In (Patient Flow) and with the 

icon in Charting and Calendar.

On the Update Appointment card, you'll see the patient's name indicated under Appointment Status.

Return to Scheduled Status

When a patient has used the Self Check In feature, but you would like to modify the check in billing details or any other reason, you can update the appointment.

For patients with a Checked In status (when Enable was checked), use Update Appointment to return this appointment to the scheduled status.

For patients with an Arrived status (Enable was not checked), use Update Appointment to edit, reschedule, confirm, return to scheduled status, cancel, or delete. 

Walk-In Appointments 

If you want to Allow patients to book walk-in appointments online, check the box to enable. 

Walk-In Appointment Online Booking

Your patients will now be able to book a Walk-In Appointment.

1.  Patients can select the type of appointment that want.  Only appointments that allow patients to schedule online will be available.

2. Patients can Select a provider.

3. Patients will be asked to log in to the Online Booking Site or create an account.

4.  Once the patient is logged in, they select BOOK MY APPOINTMENT.

5.  They will see a confirmation that they have been checked in.

6.  The patient will show up italicized in the Scheduling Calendar & Patient Flow and also in Charting to signify the patient has checked in via Online Booking.
