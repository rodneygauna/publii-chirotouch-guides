Practice Ledger - Activity 

The Activity card in the Practice Ledger displays all practice-related ledger activity, including encounter details, purchases, and transaction history for your entire practice. 

Use the filters available in each tab to narrow down your search.

Encounters

The Encounters tab displays all patient encounters and related information for your practice. Here, you can quickly see which encounters need attention from you or the provider. The display tells you if encounters need to be signed, if charges need to be posted, and how many encounters have not yet been balanced. You can view encounter details, collect payments from patients who have an outstanding balance, and view invoices. Encounters can be filtered by any of the criteria listed in the Filters section.

Encounter Details

The encounter table displays details related to the encounter, which include:

  • Patient - the patient who was seen during the encounter
  • Date - the date of the encounter
  • Type - the provider and the category of encounter
  • Encounter - the encounter number and status (click the hyperlink to see a summary of the encounter)
  • Charge Status - information about whether all, some, or none of the charges have been posted
  • Charges - the total amount of the charges, plus the invoice number(s) of the charges
  • Balance - the balance of the encounter, separated by responsible party
  • Outstanding - money that you are still owed for the encounter

Collect Payment

1.  Select the encounter action menu (ellipsis).Note: Action menu options displayed will vary based on encounter and charge status. 

2.  Select Collect Payment.

3. In the Add Payment slideout, fill out the payment details, then click POST (in blue).

4. From the Posted Payment slideout, users can:

  • Allocate Payment - allocate this payment to open related charges
  • Print Receipt- opens up printable receipt in a new tab

Create An Invoice

1.  Select the action menu (ellipsis) for the encounter from which you would like to create an invoice.

2. The newly created invoice will now appear in the line for the encounter. Click the blue invoice hyperlink to view the charges attached to the invoice.

Transaction History Details

If you click on the down arrow, the transaction will expand and show the details related to each transaction.

 Statuses

Billed Status

You can update or change any of the statuses manually. The billed status shows:

  • PT - the patient has been billed
  • P - the Primary Insurance has been billed
  • S - the Secondary Insurance has been billed

Hold Status

You can manually change the Hold Status.
