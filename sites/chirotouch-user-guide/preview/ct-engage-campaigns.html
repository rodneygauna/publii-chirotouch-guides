CT Engage - Campaigns

Create campaigns to send to your patients via email.  You can send it to a group of patients or individual patients.  The campaign feature comes pre-loaded with eight templates, but you can create your own.

NOTE: You can edit all the contents of the message, but not the images.

Create A Campaign

1. Select a campaign template to create a new campaign.

2. Pick the recipients for this campaign.  Choose from:

  • All Active Patients - select all patients that are marked as 'Active' in ChiroTouch to receive the email campaign
  • Upload Contacts - upload an Excel / CSV file of patients to receive the email campaign
  • Select Patients from Schedule - gives you the ability to send a message to segments of patients based on the appointment date
  • Individual Contacts - gives you the ability to search for patients one by one to add to the email campaign

3. You can edit the subject, title, and body of the email, and a preview of the email will be displayed on the right.

4. You can send the campaign immediately by clicking the SEND button.  You can schedule it to be sent later by clicking SCHEDULE.  Or TEST the campaign by sending a test email to registered users of your account to view and make changes before sending it to your patients.  

5. You will be asked to confirm your message before it is sent.

6. View all past campaigns and scheduled campaigns in the in Campaigns.  From here, you can preview, replicate, or cancel the campaigns and see the recipient list.

Create a Birthday Contact List

It's nice when people remember birthdays, anniversaries, and other important dates.  You can create a birthday contact CSV file using the Patient Birthdays Dashboard.

1.  You will need to delete several columns. The CSV created in Dashboard contains more information than CT Engage needs and will cause errors.  Delete columns B, C, E, and G.,  Save your new file.

2.  You will be left with a CSV that looks like this, which is the correct format for CT Engage.  Now you can upload this list and send your birthday campaign.
