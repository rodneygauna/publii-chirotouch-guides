CT Engage - Review Management

Use Review Management to keep track of your social media, including patient reviews and feedback. Patients are asked the day after their visit to provide reviews and feedback. 

Note: This feature only asks for feedback once every 60 days.

Overview

Feedback & Requests

Feedback & Requests is a running list of all the patients from whom feedback has been requested. 

If they leave you any direct feedback, the comments will be displayed in the blue box on the right.  The green box on the left lets you know what review activity your patient has done, such as visit Google.

Public Reviews

Social Media

Connect Google

1. Click Connect Google My Business.

2. You will need to be logged into the Google account that your business is connected to. You'll be asked if you'd like to continue with your current log-in.

3. Confirm what Engage is allowed.  Select Allow.

4. You will be asked to select the page(s) you'd like to connect.

Connect Facebook

1. Click Connect with Facebook.

2. If you are already logged into Facebook, you will be asked if you'd like to continue with your current log-in.

3.  You will be asked to select the page(s) you'd like to connect. Click Next.

4. Confirm what Engage is allowed.  Select Done.

5.  Select the page you'd like to connect from the list.

Review Sites

Add Review Sites to enable your practices' ability to ask for a review on customer sites.

Add Review Site

To add a review site, select Add Review Site.  You may select multiple review sites, which will be added to your email.  But it's generally best to stick with no more than 3 or 4 of the most popular sites in your area.

2. A dialog box will come up.  If you know the URL, you can paste it.  You also have the option to search for available sites to select.

2.  As an example, if you'd like your patients to leave a review on your Google page, we need to get your Google Place ID.

  • Visit https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/places/web-service/place-id
  • Scroll down the page and look for Find the ID of a particular place. Then, Enter a  location in the search bar.
  • Copy and paste the Place ID.  (You can do this to a computer notepad or word document.  You will add this to the link below.)

3. To lead your patients directly to the leave a review portion of your google business page, the link needs to be in the format https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=<place_id>

Replace <place_id> with the Place ID you just obtained for your business so that patients will be taken directly to the review link. So for our purposes, it would look like this:https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=Not7real6place4ID

4. You will paste that entire string back into the dialog box and SAVE.

5. You can see your review site has been saved.

5. Now, when you ask your patients for a review, they will be taken straight to a Google review link that looks like this.

Settings

In Settings, you'll find the Opt Out List and the ability to manage auto replies for your Google reviews.

Customize Your Responses

1. You can customize your responses when your patients leave a review.

2. To turn this feature on, you will need to check the box for Auto replies to google reviews.
