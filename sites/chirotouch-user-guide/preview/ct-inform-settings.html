CT InForms - Settings

The Settings tab allows you to change all your practice settings.

Add Form Header

To create a default header for all your forms, check the box for Enable Custom Header.

1.  Click on Header Helper.

2.  Fill out the form. 

  • Add your contact information
  • Upload and resize your logo
  • Choose a background and text colors
  • Add your social media links

3.  Preview your choices and select Use this header.

PDF Header

You can set up headers for your PDF forms.

Emails

Change your email templates. See a preview your changes as your customers would.

SMS

Create customized SMS messages to send with your templates.

Settings

Set your preferences.   

If you add links to your forms on a website, you can update the Public Form Login Method to require an email or phone number for confirmation. This method offers three advantages: 

  • Authenticating the user email/phone helps validate the patient's identity 
  • It prevents spammers from filling out your forms 
  • The client can come back to complete an unfinished form at anytime 

The drawback of enabling this confirmation is that now your patients will need to perform an extra step to fill out your form. 

Email Sender

You can set up the email address your patient’s see when you email forms to them.

Notifications

Choose which notifications you receive.
