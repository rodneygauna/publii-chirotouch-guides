Patient Record - Overview 

The Overview tab in the patient's profile consists of 2 subsections: Profile and Appointments. 

Profile Tab

In the Profile section, users can view, add, and edit:

  • Demographics
  • Alerts & Notes
  • Insurance
  • Active Guarantors
  • Online Booking Link

Update Patient Demographics

Patient demographics can be updated from various areas within the application. 

1. To edit or update a patient's existing demographics, click anywhere within the Demographics section outlined below.

2.  Once clicked, the Update Patient slideout will appear. Update the Basic Information, including photo.  Add any Additional Information as needed.

3.  Complete the patient's address (including their 9-digit zip code), phone number, and/or email address. Once edits or updates are made, select Save (in blue).

Note: 9-digit zip codes are required for electronic billing.

Note:  If you add a patient's Social Security Number, and the number already exists in ChiroTouch, you will receive a message that there is a duplicate.

Inactive Patient

If you need to inactivate a patient, you can use the patient record to reflect that change and remove them from your patient roster.

1. Pull up the patient that you wish to make inactive.  Use the toggle at the bottom of  Update Patient to make them inactive.

2.  Once you make the patient Inactive, a checkbox and a date box will appear. If you check this box and add the Date of Death, the information will be used to populate FL19 for paper and electronic claims.

3.  The patient will be shown as in inactive across their picture.

4.  You may toggle Inactive patients on and off using the ellipsis on the patient roster.

Add Or Update Patient Alerts & Notes

  • Notes - will display the note entered in by the user within the Patients Profile unless removed

View Patient Insurance 

The Insurance section will display any active insurances added into the patient chart from the Insurance section. Below you will learn how to view and update patient insurances.

1.  To view, add, or update a patient's insurance, click into the section outlined below

2.  Users will be routed to the Insurance section of the Patient Record.  Click here to learn more about Insurance.

View Active Guarantors

The Active Guarantor section will display all active Guarantors added into the patient chart from the Billing section. Below you will learn how to view and update Guarantors.

1.  To view, add or update a patient's Guarantors, click anywhere within the Guarantor section outlined below.

2.  Users will be routed to the Billing section of the Patient Record. Click here to learn more about Billing.

Online Booking Link

In the Online Booking Link section, users can send an invitation email to the patient, providing them access to your Online Booking site. 

1.  Select the Send Invitation Email to Patient (in blue).

2.  From the Send Invitation Email dialog, select an email or add a new email address.

3.  The customer will get an invite in their email.

4.  When the customer clicks on Create Account, they will be directed to your online booking portal.

5.  After a patient schedules an appointment online, it will show up in scheduling.  When you click the appointment, you will seen the following prompt about the appointment.  You can then take the appropriate action to Match patient or Create New Patient Account.

6.  If you are in Charting and the patient does not have a profile yet, the patient will appear as inactive.  You will be prompted to create a new account by returning to Scheduling.

Appointments Tab

In the Appointments section, users can add and view future and past appointments. 

Add New Appointment

1.  From the Appointments tab, select Add New Appointment (in blue).

2.  From the Add New Appointment slideout, select:

  • Provider
  • Appointment Type
  • Case - not required in the system, but adding one here, if applicable, is essential to avoiding billing issues later
  • Authorization - not required, but adding it to this appointment, if applicable, could help with billing later

3.  Choose the Appointment Type:

  • One Time Appointment
  • Recurring Series

4.  Select by Date, Time, or Override.

5.  Choose the Duration in Hours and Minutes.

6.  Once updates are made, select Schedule to save the appointment.

View Future & Past Appointments

In the Appointments section, users can add appointments and view future and past ones for this patient.

1.  Click on Appointments.

2.  Clicking on the appointment date and time hyperlink will route the user to the Update Appointment slideout where users can:

  • Update Appointment
    • Edit
    • Reschedule
    • Cancel
    • Delete 
  • View appointment details 

Note: The blue Edit Patient icon is located throughout the app. Clicking on it, where it's available, will allow users to quickly view and update demographic information.
