Check Out

Patient Check Out is the last opportunity for interaction with the patient before they depart from your practice, it's important to make sure they leave fully satisfied with their visit. Payments collected during Check Out will automatically be allocated to posted encounter charges. 

Check Out Patient

1.  From Scheduling, select the colored patient appointment card you wish to Check Out.

2.  From the Check Out slideout, select Check Out (in blue).

3.  The Check Out slideout will open.  View the Encounter Balance below the appointment details. 

4.  Select the Billing Details, which include:

  • Self Pay - payment falls under the responsibility of the patient  or designated Guarantor
  • Care Package - choose (or add) a care package/subscription associated with the encounter
  • Insurance - payment falls under the responsibility of the patient's elected insurance payer

Note:  To update the patient's guarantor, select the Guarantor dropdown. To elect a new one, select Add Patient Guarantor (in blue).

5.  Select Make Payment (in blue).

6.  From the Add Patient Payment slideout, fill in the fields below:

  • Payment Type - select a code related to patient or payer payments
  • Amount - payment amount of the payment type selected
  • Payment Method - cash, check, debit, or credit card 
  • Transaction Date 

Note: Payment Types first need to be created in Billing Codes under Payment Types. 

7.  Once the payment fields are completed, select Post (in blue).

8. Once a payment has successfully posted, a green success banner will display, and the user will be presented with the Posted Payment details, allowing them to perform various actions.

  • Void - void the payment
  • Allocate Payment - allocate that payment toward a specific charge
  • Print Receipt - print a PDF receipt

View Next Appointment Details

Providers can request the next appointment from Charting. Click here to learn more about Charting. 

1.  Next appointment details will display in the Request Next Appointment.

2. To schedule the next appointment, select Add New Appointment (in blue). 

Encounter Diagnosis and Charges

1.  At the bottom of Check Out slideout, the encounter diagnoses and charges are displayed.

2.  If charges have not been posted, you can Post (in blue) or Delete (blue minus sign), add Modifiers, and select Diagnosis Pointers in the Check Out slideout.

Note:  Charges deleted from the Check Out screen will remain as part of the encounter as unposted. Complete removal on a charge may only be done on the encounter.

3.  When you have finished, Complete Check Out (in blue). 

4.  Once the patient has successfully been Checked Out, a green success banner will display and the Appointment Status will update. To return to Scheduling, select the X  (top right corner). 

Add New Charge

1. If at Check Out, you notice a charge is missing from the encounter, you can Add New Charge.

2.  The Add Charge - Encounter slideout will appear.  Click Add Charge (in blue).

3.   Select the procedure and Add Charge, followed by DONE.

4.  The charge will be added to the charge details.  From here you can add a diagnosis pointer or delete (minus sign) if you made a mistake. Click POST when finished.
