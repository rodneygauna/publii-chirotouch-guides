Practice - Claims Worklist

ChiroTouch provides robust functionality for billers to manage claims, whether they are submitting on paper, electronically, or are in the process of transitioning to e-claims.  Billers can submit, print, resubmit, cancel, and manage statuses from an individual claim or from the Claims Worklist, where bulk actions are available for some functionality.  In addition, the Claims Worklist allows for easy management of claims throughout their lifecycle.

Mapping Claim Fields

If you need assistance as your work through your claim submissions with populating fields (ex: 9a or 24d), check out the list of how claim fields are mapped.

Claim Categories

The Claims Worklist is a convenient way for billers to manage all claims, whether they are paper or electronic submissions. The Claims Worklist allows users to view a snapshot of claims in categories: Ready To Submit, Needs Attention, Submitted, Denied & Rejected, and Approved.  NOTE: Claims that are canceled, do not display in the Claims Worklist.
