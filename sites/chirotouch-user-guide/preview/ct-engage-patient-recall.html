CT Engage - Patient Recall

Patient Recall searches for the patients that are overdue for care.

Settings

Recall Settings are pulled from the Appointments in ChiroTouch.  They are defaulted OFF (unchecked) and to 6 months Time Since Last Visit.

Daily Review

Due dates are based on the last time the patient had a specific appointment type with the practice.  

  • If no appointment is scheduled:
    • Upcoming Due - 30 days prior to the next appointment 'due date'
    • Past Due -  1 day after an appointment 'due date'
