Patient Ledger - Balance 

The Balance card in the Patient Record displays the patient's balance for both patient and payer-related charges and payments. Here, only open charges will display, allowing you to balance them accordingly. In addition to balancing open charges, you have the ability to created Patient Statements and Superbills.

Use the filters to narrow your search. The Date of Service automatically defaults to the last 30 days.

Summary

The Summary section from within the Balance card displays a Charge Balance table, with the patient's total charges and payments created and any open charges to be balanced. To learn how to balance open charges, click here to learn about charges.

Statements

The Statements section from within the Balance card allows users to generate, preview and track Patient Statements. You may filter results to quickly find a statement.

  • Statement ID - search by a specific statement number
  • Date of Service - add a date range
  • Guarantor - search by name
  • Balance - add a minimum and/or maximum balance amount (Not available under closed tab)

Create Patient Statements

1.  From the Balance Card, select Statements, followed by Generate New Statement (in blue). 

2.  From the Generate New Statement dialog, select from the following options:

  • Outstanding Charges Only - will only display outstanding charges on the patient statement 
  • All Charges - will display all charges on the patient statement
  • Date Range - select a date range of charges or services rendered if desired 
  • Providers - select all or individual providers
  • Include Previous Balance - will include a balance forward line on the patient statement
  • Include Aging Table - displays an aging table of charges in 30, 60, and 90-day increments 
  • Generate - opens the Patient Statement in a new tab to print, while logging in into their account
  • Preview - opens the Patient Statement in a new tab to print, but does not log it into their account

Note: Previous balance is the total of all outstanding patient balances for services rendered before the "Date of Service From" selection above. To include a previous balance on the patient statement, select a "Date of Service" range.

3.  Select GENERATE (in blue).

4. Once Generated, the patient statement will open up in a new tab to print, and also display in the patient's chart.

Group by Relationship

Statements can be generated for patients that share relationships.

1. From Generate New Statement, select the checkbox for Group by Relationship. 

2. Select the Related Patients you want to Generate a statement.

3. A PDF will open in a new window with All Related Patients selected grouped together on one statement with their charges separated out individually.

4. At the bottom of the group statement, you'll see the total of all charges and payments for the Related Patient Statement.

Superbills

The Superbills section from within the Balance card allows users to generate, preview, and track patient superbills. Use the filters to quickly find what you need.

  • Superbill ID - enter the Superbill number if it's known
  • Date of Service - add a date range
  • Payers - select the person or entity paying the bill
  • Providers - select one or more providers 
  • Total Charges - add a minimum and/or maximum amount

Create Patient Superbills

1.  From the Balance Card, select Superbills, followed by Generate New Superbill (in blue). 

2.  From the Generate New Superbill dialog, select the following options:

  • Date of Service Range - select a date range of charges or services rendered if desired 
  • Payers - choose from the Payers associated with the patient's charges, including Self Pay customers 
  • Unique Superbill Per Payer - check the box to create a unique Superbill per payer selected in the payers dropdown
  • Providers - select all or individual providers
  • Include charges without a patient balance - if selected, includes charges without a patient balance on the Superbill
  • Include zero dollars - if selected, include charges that equal $0
  • Include Adjustments - if selected, includes adjustments 
  • Generate - opens the Patient Superbill in a new tab to print, while logging in to their account
  • Preview - opens the Patient Superbill in a new tab to print, but does not log it into their account

Note: Select individual payers to produce Superbills listing only payer-specific charges, for each selection. A unique Superbill per payer will be created. Choose All Payers and deselect Unique Superbill Per Payer to produce a Superbill listing any matching charges.

3. Select GENERATE (in blue) to create a superbill.

4. Once Generated, the patient superbill will open up in a new tab to print. 

5. The Superbill will be available to view and print later.

Missing DX Pointers

If you generate a Superbill and the DX Pointers are not present on the charges, you will need to edit the charge line in the ledger.  If an encounter has been signed, the encounter will need to be reopened to add the diagnoses to the charges.
